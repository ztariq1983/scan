How it works
1. Load Opening Range — after 9:35 AM ET, fetches the first 5-minute bar from Alpaca and records each ticker's opening high, low, and volume.
2. Start Live Scan — polls live quotes from Finnhub every 30 seconds. Flags when price breaks above the opening high with volume confirmation.
Status key: Waiting before 9:35 · Inside within range · Approaching within 0.3% of high · 🚀 Breakout above high with volume
